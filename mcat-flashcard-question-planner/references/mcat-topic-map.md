# MCAT Topic Map (Planning Level)

Use this as a balancing guide when distributing content.

## CP (Chemical and Physical Foundations)

- General chemistry: equilibrium, thermodynamics, kinetics, acids/bases, electrochemistry.
- Physics: mechanics, fluids, circuits, waves, optics, atomic/nuclear basics.
- Biochemistry overlaps: amino acids, enzyme behavior, metabolism fundamentals.

## CARS (Critical Analysis and Reasoning Skills)

- Main idea and thesis identification.
- Author tone, stance, and intent.
- Inference under uncertainty.
- Argument structure and evidence evaluation.

## BB (Biological and Biochemical Foundations)

- Molecular biology: DNA/RNA, replication, transcription, translation, regulation.
- Cell biology: organelles, signaling, transport, cell cycle.
- Systems physiology: nervous, endocrine, cardiovascular, respiratory, renal, immune.
- Biochemistry depth: pathways, protein structure/function, lab interpretation.

## PS (Psychological, Social, and Biological Foundations)

- Psychology: learning, memory, cognition, emotion, behavior.
- Sociology: groups, stratification, institutions, demographics.
- Research methods and interpretation of behavioral data.

## Difficulty bands

- Foundational: direct concept recall and basic application.
- Medium: multi-step reasoning with familiar contexts.
- Exam-like: integration across topics, subtle distractors, time pressure.
